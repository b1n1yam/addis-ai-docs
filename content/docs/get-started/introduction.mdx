## Why Addis AI?

Building AI for Ethiopia is different. Generic models from Silicon Valley treat our languages as an edge case. We treat them as the core product.

We solve the **three hardest problems** in Ethiopian NLP:

1.  **Morphology:** Amharic verbs fuse subject, object, and tense into single words. Our models parse this correctly where GPT-4 often hallucinates.
2.  **Culture:** Our models know the history of Adwa, the geography of Oromia, and the current legal frameworks of Ethiopia.
3.  **Voice:** We don't just do text. We built the most natural sounding TTS engines for Amharic and Afan Oromo.
